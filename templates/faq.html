Common Questions

How do I place an order? +
Browse our menu on the home page. When you find something you like, simply click it to add it to your cart. Once you are ready, click the 'Cart' button in the top right corner and head to the checkout page where you can submit your delivery details.
What are your delivery zones and times? +
We deliver anywhere within a 15-mile radius of downtown Food City. Delivery operations run from 10:00 AM to 11:30 PM, seven days a week. Expect most orders to arrive within 30-45 minutes!
What is your refund policy? +
If your food arrives cold, damaged, or if we missed an item, please contact us immediately at help@burgerhouse.com or call us at +91 9527345127. We will issue a full refund or send a replacement right away.
Where can I find allergen information? +
We take allergies seriously! Our kitchen processes dairy, gluten, soy, and nuts. Every menu item description gives a brief overview of ingredients. If you have a severe allergy, please call us directly before placing your order so we can make safe accommodations.
What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept Cash on Delivery (COD), Online via UPI, Net Banking, and Credit/Debit cards. You can securely select your preferred method on the checkout page.
